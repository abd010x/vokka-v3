Payment held securely by our banking partner until you approve. Artisans cannot access full payment until you're satisfied.

Fair to artisans

40% upfront covers fabric and production. They quote their own price. Artizan's 15% is added on top, never taken from theirs.

Disputes resolved

Minor fixes re-done at no cost. Major issues get a refund. Artizan mediates every case. Three-strike quality policy.

Buyers: Your review releases the artisan's final payment. A quick review means your artisan gets paid faster — we'll nudge you gently so they're not left waiting.
